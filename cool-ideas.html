-The Universe-

What if the universe has many crusts, and we're living on one of the empty areas in between the layers? They could be made out of something like force fields. If the crusts had lots of gravity, that could also explain whey the universe is expanding. Earth would be a small planet in between one of the crusts.

What if...we don't exist? What if there seems to be no creatures in the universe except for humans because we're actually just a program or game that aliens made for entertainment? Then all the facts we've lived based on could be lies. So how do we know if we really exist or not?
